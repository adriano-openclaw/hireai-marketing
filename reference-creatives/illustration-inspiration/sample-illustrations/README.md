## Style rules for future generated illustrations

- Clean vector / hand-drawn hybrid corporate illustration.
- Bold black outlines with slight organic irregularity; thinner interior detail strokes.
- Minimal palette: white/off-white fills, dark charcoal/black details, HireAI orange accents, occasional pale peach/orange background blob.
- Flat fills, minimal shading, no photorealistic lighting, no 3D, no painterly texture.
- Friendly B2B SaaS/editorial tone; business, sales, recruiting, data, reporting, productivity, and automation themes.
- Characters: simple rounded faces, dot/oval eyes, curved brows, small noses, expressive mouths, solid black hair, business attire.
- Objects: laptops, tablets, dashboards, bar charts, emails, reports, folders, paper stacks, pens, notebooks, clipboards, phones, mugs, clocks, lightbulbs.
- Composition: isolated vignette, centered clear silhouette, generous negative space, transparent background preferred; use a soft pale orange organic blob only when helpful.
- Screens/documents should use abstract lines and charts, not readable text.

## Image-generation prompt recipe

Use this structure when generating a new HireAI illustration:

> Create a clean vector-style corporate illustration in the HireAI visual style: bold hand-drawn black outlines, flat white/off-white fills, dark charcoal details, and vivid HireAI orange accents. Show [subject/action] involving [office/recruiting/sales/productivity objects]. Use simplified cartoon proportions, expressive minimal facial features, solid black hair shapes, and clean business attire. Include simple props such as [laptop/tablet/charts/emails/documents/coffee/phone] with abstract lines and bar charts, no readable text. Composition should be centered with clear negative space, isolated on a transparent background, optionally with a soft pale orange organic blob behind the subject. Minimal shading; no photorealism, no 3D, no gradients, no detailed background, no anime/comic rendering.
